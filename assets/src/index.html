About Us

Our specialized service

In the German-speaking world, DESIGNERDOCK is THE personnel consultancy for the communications industry. Since our founding in 1996, we’ve helped find permanent and freelance staff for more than 3000 companies – including marketing, mobile, PR, digital, advertising and design agencies. Whether temporary or permanent, for a single task or a whole campaign, we’ll find seasoned specialists or an entire team of professionals to suit your needs.

This is how we work

Together with our client, we formulate a precise job profile. We identify candidates in our network database and through research who match the profile. Our personnel consultants evaluate the results. Appropriate candidates are then contacted and reviewed again with specific reference to the position. When this is done, we send the client a selection of suitable and available candidates.
